For our typography, we used one pixel sans-serif font which is pixeloid-Sans. Since our game is a pixel design, we chose to use pixel font to make the two consistent. Pixeloid-Sans is our game's main font, used for heading, buttons, quiz and dialogues. It has both general and bold font styles, and the bold font is used to emphasize some important or special content.
