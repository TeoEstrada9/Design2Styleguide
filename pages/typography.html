For our typography, we used to pixel sans-serif fonts, pixeloid-mono and pixeloid-sans. Since our game is a pixel design, we chose to use pixel fonts to make the two consistent. Pixeloid-san is our game's main font, used for heading, buttons and dialogues. It has both general and bold font styles, and the bold font is used to emphasize some important or special content. Pixeloid-mono is used for styleguide pages and “Survey” part in our game.
