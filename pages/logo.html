Main logo
The circular logo of "Made in the Waves" with waves, and the whale symbolizes natural beauty, harmony, and sustainability in ocean conservation. 

It conveys a sense of interconnectedness, wholeness, and the importance of protecting marine life.


Variations
The stroke outline variations of the "Made in the Waves" logo in green, red, and yellow provide a simplified, versatile, and colorful representation.
