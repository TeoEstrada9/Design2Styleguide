Why we chose this

We decided to go with a pixel design because we
Wanted to give off an arcade feel and make it stand out from
the other apps that people created, and attract kids and other people
who enjoy those 80's arcade type games for when they have some spare
time on their hands!

Our Icons and buttons will be around 60-70px so they are big enough
for users to click on it but not to big that it takes up a lot of
space on the page. To have a easy straightforward navigation

The buttons will be around 45-50px or less so they are secondary
buttons and wont take up to much space on the screen and it is still
clickable for the users.
