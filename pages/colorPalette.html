Under the Waves Colour Palette

For our web app, we chose a colourful palette to freely illustrate the ocean, nature and the sea creatures that come in an abundance of hues and tints. With the bright colourful palette we have, we hope for users to feel excitement and happiness for adventure and learning.

Examples

The Colours "Coral"and "Salmon"are used for various things like, seashells, octopus and various other sea creatures and coral reefs. The "Coral" colour is also used for the marketplace icon.

The yellows: "Straw Yellow" & "Hazelnut" are used for sand, treasure, fishes and the beach icon. "Pale White" would mainly be used for backgrounds or sand.

The "Seaweed Green" and "Turtle Green" are mainly used for plants, nature and turtles. The survey icon also uses the colour "Turtle Green".

The "Ocean Blue" and "Sky Blue" colours are of course used for the sky, ocean and underwater animals.

Lastly "Dark Brown" and other shades of brown will be used for the main character.Certain texts, dialogue, or items in the game can also be brown.
